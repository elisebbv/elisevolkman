Editing Services

You've put hours, months, even years into writing your story. Don't lose your reader on the first page due to typos or lack of focus.

Elise offers substantive, stylistic, copy editing, and proofreading for a variety of genres and projects. She particularly enjoys:

  • Fantasy
  • Science Fiction
  • Young Adult Sci-Fi/Fantasy
  • Middle Grade Sci-Fi/Fantasy
  • Memoirs
  • Cookbooks
  • Children's Picture Books

For more information, please contact Elise with these details about your manuscript: title and genre, brief summary, approximate word count, and a 5-10 page sample.

Rates calculated on a per-project basis.

Structural/Substantive

A Structural Edit provides the writer with feedback on the big-picture: everything from character development and plot structure to style, pacing, and continuity. The writer will receive (1) an Editorial Letter outlining the main feedback beats, and (2) a copy of their manuscript with comments and reflections recorded throughout. Some copy edits may be included, but this will not be the primary focus.

Stylistic

A Stylistic Edit will provide the writer with feedback on issues of language, such as word choice, jargon, wordiness, phrasing, pacing, and some structural elements. The writer will receive (1) an Editorial Letter outlining the main feedback beats, and (2) a copy of their manuscript with comments and suggestions recorded throughout. Some copy edits may be included, but this will not be the primary focus.

Copy Editing

A Copy Edit provides the writer with feedback on correctness, accuracy, and consistency. The writer will receive a copy of their manuscript with comments and corrections on grammar, spelling, style, and punctuation. Some suggestions for rewriting or reorganizing may be included, but this will not be the primary focus.

Proofreading

A Proofread is the final stage after the design has been finalized and before the book is published. The writer will receive a PDF of their manuscript with comments and corrections on any remain errors such as typos, layout issues, or other errors introduced at any point of the writing, editing, or design process.

For more information, please contact Elise with these details about your manuscript: title and genre, brief summary, approximate word count, and a 5-10 page sample.

Rates calculated on a per-project basis.
