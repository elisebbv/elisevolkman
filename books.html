Roots of Blood

Book I of the Nymph Keepers

Coming June 2021, Roots of Blood is my debut novel, the first in a dark epic fantasy series.

Nym’s people have been hunted to near-extinction for the healing properties in their blood. Jay will stop at nothing to get the blood his sister so desperately needs.

Jay, a desperate mercenary, has abducted the last living nymph of the province. She’s supposed to be his solution to everything. Instead, he ends up on the run with her and the sister he promised to save with her legendary blood. Jay has to admit that their only option is to return home from exile. He’ll risk imprisonment — or even death — in order to keep them all safe.

Nym knows she can’t trust Jay even after he saves her from the Nymph-Keeper’s dungeon. She has the scars to prove it. But she has no choice. She must put her trust in her enemies and gamble with her life. It’s the only way to retake control of her own blood and her fate.
