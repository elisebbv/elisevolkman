Elise Volkman

Writer | Editor

Elise offers substantive, stylistic, and copy editing for a variety of genres and projects. She particularly enjoys fantasy and science fiction, but also has experience with memoirs, cookbooks, and children's picture books.

A sentence may be perfectly correct in terms of grammar, and still be perfectly boring or confusing to read. An editor should be able to see this, know it, and offer suggestions to improve it rather than prescribing a one-size-fits-all answer.
